Indentured Goblin Accountant

A handy goblin to help allocate your hard earned loot

Adventurers

Enter comma separated names of players in the "Adventurers" box.
eg. Acererak, Bigby, Conan

Hard earned loot

Enter comma separated items of loot in the "Hard earned loot" box.
eg. gold ring 25gp, diamond 100gp, 50sp

Use the following abbreviations for coinage: cp, sp, ep, gp, pp

No space between number and money values
eg. 15gp

Items should have a format of name then value:
eg. gold ring 25gp, wooden rocking horse with silver inlay and quartz eyes 25gp
